WHAT DID WE LEARN FROM COVID-19?

We have learned a lot from the horrible, sad and traumatic experiences that were forced upon us by this pandemic. It affected each of us on a very personal level. The effects were not equally divided among us.

Some lost much more than others. In spite of these painful circumstances, some people actually profited. But, please understand – Profiting from the misfortune of others is definitely NOT what this is about! Quite the opposite.

NOTE:   Huge numbers of businesses were forced to shut their doors. Promising start-ups were delayed or cancelled. Hopes and dreams were shattered for many.

However, even in the midst and the aftermath of this widespread pain and suffering, there are vast numbers of exciting business opportunities available. In fact, there are even more of them now than there were before Covid-19 arrived. The key is knowing how to properly locate, evaluate and acquire the best opportunities.

Our special business system was already deep into development long before Covid-19, but it is now close to its worldwide launch. But because we have seen how many businesses were affected by Covid-19, we restructured our launch parameters. We now have made some special changes to our system and how we plan to launch.

We believe that, because of Covid, when we finally initiate our worldwide launch announcement, there will likely be much larger numbers of entrepreneurs interested in our program than we initially estimated. So, if this is correct, we need to adjust.

We are excited about that possibility, but we need a different launch plan. We want instead to first identify a limited number of entrepreneurs to receive early access to our program before our official launch. Instead, we want to train this chosen group.

In other words, we want to work closely with a limited group of serious entrepreneurs before the launch. We will give them early access to our entire system and then interact with them to help them become “experts” well before the official launch.

To locate and select these entrepreneurs, we have designed a plan. Once we have selected and trained the required number of serious entrepreneurs needed, we will again move forward toward our global launch.

These entrepreneurs will also assist us, as part of their training, to evaluate and modify several very advanced features of our system. So, realizing that we need their help with these advanced features, we want to be sure that they will be focused.
